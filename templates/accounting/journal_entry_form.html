Quick Guide — How Debits & Credits Work

DEBIT (DR) — choose DR when you want to:

  • ↑ Increase an Asset (Cash, Loans Receivable, Equipment)
  • ↑ Increase an Expense account
  • ↓ Decrease a Liability or Income

CREDIT (CR) — choose CR when you want to:

  • ↑ Increase a Liability (Savings Deposits, Borrowings)
  • ↑ Increase Income / Revenue
  • ↓ Decrease an Asset or Expense

Example — recording a ₦10,000 loan disbursement: DR Loans Receivable ₦10,000 and CR Cash Account ₦10,000. Total Debits must always equal Total Credits.
